Citrus Hack 2016


Voice Controlled Electronics Board

We (group of four) created a circuit board controlled by your voice: you assign a voice command for all/some of the Raspberry Pi's 16 GPIO pins, and can then turn them on/off using your voice! Our presentation used a proof-of-concept variant with three LEDs, however this board can be extended to voice control various aspects of your home!




Cutie Hack 2017


Three Cup Shuffle Solver

We (group of four) created a robot that tracks cups in the classic three cup shuffle game, at the end telling you which cup the ball is under! We used OpenCV, an Arduino, and a $6 camera, programming the logic and vision processing in Python. The camera is situated above the table, at the viewer's position; you press a button to mark which cup the ball is initially under, and the tracking begins. We created several motion tracking algorithms, compensating for the camera's low frame rate.
