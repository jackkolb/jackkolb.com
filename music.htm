Music


My music career began when I was four, and continued formally until high school graduation: I studied with a number of teachers, ultimately at the San Domenico Music Conservatory enrolled in the Music Teacher Association of California's Certificate of Merit program. In my graduating year, I completed CM's Advanced Level. I have received instruction in piano, violin, and clarinet, however most affiliate with the piano.
